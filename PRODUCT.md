# Product Context

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

Static browser tool for French admissions juries. It helps evaluators capture structured notes during an interview, track time, and generate a concise final assessment.

## Users

Admissions jury members using a laptop or tablet during candidate interviews. They need speed, clarity, predictable controls, and low distraction.

## Principles

- Keep the interview workflow visible on the first screen.
- Favor dense but readable form controls over decorative layout.
- Make candidate data hard to lose accidentally.
- Be explicit about API key handling and generation errors.
- Support mobile and tablet layouts without horizontal scrolling.

## Tone

Professional, factual, calm, task-focused.
